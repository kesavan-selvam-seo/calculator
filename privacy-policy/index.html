Privacy Policy

At CalcuPortal, we respect your privacy and aim to keep our website simple, useful, and transparent. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our calculators, browse our pages, contact us, or interact with features available on our website.

By using CalcuPortal, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please avoid using the website or submitting any information through our contact form.

Information We Do Not Store from Calculators

Most calculators on CalcuPortal work directly inside your browser. This means the values you enter into our calculator tools, such as numbers, dates, ages, weights, financial figures, percentages, or conversion values, are processed on your device for generating the result.

We do not intentionally collect, save, or store the personal calculation inputs you enter into our tools. Your calculator inputs are used only to display the result on the page and are not submitted to us as part of normal calculator usage.

Information You May Share with Us

If you contact us through our contact form or email address, we may receive the information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, subject, message, calculator page URL, feedback details, or correction request. We use this information only to respond to your inquiry, review feedback, fix issues, or improve the website.

Please do not send sensitive personal information through our contact form, such as passwords, banking details, government identification numbers, private health records, or confidential documents.

Cookies and Local Preferences

CalcuPortal may use cookies or local browser storage to improve your browsing experience. For example, we may store your theme preference, such as light mode or dark mode, so the website can remember your preferred display setting during future visits.

You can disable cookies or clear browser storage through your browser settings. However, some website preferences may not work properly if cookies or local storage are disabled.

Analytics and Basic Usage Data

We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use CalcuPortal. These tools may collect basic information such as visited pages, device type, browser type, general location, referral source, session duration, and interaction patterns. This information helps us improve website performance, content quality, navigation, and calculator usability.

Analytics data is used in an aggregated manner and is not intended to personally identify individual users. We use this information to understand which calculators are useful, which pages need improvement, and how we can make the website easier to use.

Advertising and Third-Party Services

CalcuPortal may display advertisements or use third-party advertising services in the future. Advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to show relevant ads, measure ad performance, and prevent abuse or invalid activity.

Third-party services may collect certain information according to their own privacy policies. CalcuPortal does not control how third-party advertisers or external services handle data, so we recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more details.

External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites, references, tools, or third-party resources. Once you leave CalcuPortal and visit another website, we are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, security, or policies of that external website.

We encourage users to read the privacy policy of any external website they visit, especially before submitting personal information.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep CalcuPortal secure and reliable. However, no website, online service, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users should always be careful while sharing information online.

Since our calculators are designed mainly for general informational and estimation purposes, users should avoid entering highly sensitive or confidential information into any online calculator.

Children’s Privacy

CalcuPortal is designed for general users and educational purposes. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has submitted personal information through our website, they can contact us and request removal of that information where applicable.

CalcuPortal provides calculators for quick estimates, learning, and general informational use. Calculator results should not be treated as professional, financial, medical, legal, tax, or official advice.
