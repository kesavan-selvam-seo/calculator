Custom Lottery Number Range

Different lottery games and random draws may use different number ranges. Some games may use numbers from 1 to 49, while others may use 1 to 60, 1 to 70 or any custom range. This generator allows you to set your own range so the output matches your requirement.

For example, if you want six numbers between 1 and 49, enter 1 as the minimum value, 49 as the maximum value and 6 as the number count. The tool will generate six unique random numbers within that range.

Does the Tool Repeat Numbers?

No. The Lottery Number Generator creates unique numbers in a single generated set. If the number count is 6, the tool will generate six different numbers without repeating the same value in that ticket.

To avoid errors, the selected number count must be smaller than or equal to the total available numbers in the selected range. For example, if your range is 1 to 5, you cannot generate 6 unique numbers.
