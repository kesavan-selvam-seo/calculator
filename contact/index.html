Contact CalcuPortal

Have a question, suggestion, or correction request? We would be happy to hear from you. CalcuPortal is built to make everyday calculations easier, and user feedback plays an important role in improving our calculators, content, page experience, and overall accuracy.

Whether you are using our finance calculators, health tools, math calculators, conversion utilities, date tools, or student-friendly calculators, you can contact us if something needs attention or if you want to suggest a new tool that could help more users.

Send Us a Message

Use the contact form below to send feedback, report a calculator issue, suggest a new tool, or contact us for advertising and collaboration inquiries. Please add clear details so we can understand your request better.

How We Can Help

You can reach out to CalcuPortal for calculator suggestions, formula corrections, page errors, broken links, content improvements, usability feedback, advertising inquiries, and general questions related to the website. We review feedback carefully and use it to improve the quality of our tools.

If you are reporting a calculator issue, please share the calculator page URL, the values you entered, the result shown on the page, and the result you expected. This helps us understand the issue clearly and review the calculation faster.

Suggest a New Calculator

CalcuPortal is continuously expanding with new calculators and online tools. If you need a calculator that is not currently available on our website, you can send us your suggestion. We welcome ideas related to finance, health, education, math, unit conversions, date calculations, business utilities, and daily life tools.

When suggesting a new calculator, please include a short explanation of what the calculator should do and who it will help. If possible, you can also share the formula, example inputs, or expected output format.

Report an Error or Correction

We work to keep our calculator pages useful and easy to understand. However, if you notice a spelling issue, outdated explanation, incorrect formula reference, layout problem, or calculation mismatch, please let us know. Clear correction requests help us maintain a better experience for all users.

Before You Use Our Calculators

CalcuPortal provides calculators and informational content for general guidance, learning, and quick estimation. The results shown by our tools should not be treated as professional advice. For financial, medical, tax, legal, academic, or business decisions, please verify results with an official source or a qualified professional.
